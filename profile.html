Biography

Born in Ishikawa, Japan in 1995. Lived in the State of Michigan in America through the age of 11 to 16. Attended high school and university in Japan. Graduated Waseda University with a Bachelor of Engineering specialized in Electronic and Physical Systems. The undergraduate study was about deep learning in the field of image captioning with generative models. Concluded my studies with a thesis titled “Study on Natural Language Translation via Images”. Started career life in SoftBank Corp. in 2018 - present as IT Engineer. Devoted to the development of multiple SaaS products.
